🚀 Project Overview
This dashboard provides a detailed view of retail operations by visualizing:
Sales performance across cities and product lines
Profit distribution by region, gender, and payment method
Customer behavior and transaction patterns
It is designed to help businesses make data-driven decisions efficiently.

📌 Key Metrics
📦 Total Quantities Sold: 2064
🧾 Total Transactions: 2064
💰 Total Sales: 659.27K
⭐ Average Rating: 7.20
💵 Average Unit Price: 55.97
📈 Total Profit: 31.39K

📊 Dashboard Features
🔹 Sales Analysis
Sales by City (Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad)
Sales by Branch
Sales by Product Line
🔹 Profit Analysis
Profit by Region
Profit by Payment Method (Cash, E-wallet, Credit Card)
Profit by Gender & City
🔹 Customer Insights
Sales by Customer Type (Normal vs Member)
Gender-based filtering
🔹 Time Analysis
Profit trend over Date

🎯 Key Insights
📈 Highest sales come from Food & Beverages category
💳 Majority of profit is generated through Cash payments
🏙️ Cities like Mumbai contribute significantly to revenue
👥 Member customers contribute more to overall sales
📉 Profit trend shows slight variation over time
